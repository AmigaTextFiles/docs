How to get it working

There are two ways to get your APPE working:

(1) You can order it by mail to me for only 16£, 48dm, or 24 euros. If you buy it you obtain two benefices, first a quality assembled inferface and second the upgrades to versions 2 and 3 are for free, simply sending me the interface and the mail cost.

(2) Also you can build your own one, but you need some electronical aknowledge, some ability and the components listed in the archive APPE_SQ.GIF, these components are cheap and simple to find except the Pad connector, the best way to obtain it is buying a pad extensor cable on any console shop, and cutting the right connector. The heart of APPE is the PIC16F84 processor that needs the file APPE1_2.HEX to run.

If you decide to mount it yourself this is GIFTWARE, you must send me a gift. but if you decide to build lots of interfaces to sell it and win money (ej any Amiga hardware manufacturer) please do a mention of the original author and send me a percentage from the amount. :) Think that you're getting money with my idea and my work.
