The A4000T Loudspeaker Enhancer

(Deutscher Text siehe unten)

This is a horribly simple hardware hack to improve the loudspeaker
output in the A4000T. In this computer, the loudspeaker is mounted
free in the air, so it suffers from acoustic shortcuts. To avoid
this, it is enough to put some plate around it, which can simply
be made of cardboard. The file loudspeaker.iff is a drawing. Take
a piece of not too thick cardboard and cut it out after the drawing.
The radial cuts from the inner to the outer circle are there to
make tabs. Bend every other tab up or down, so that you can fix the
cardboard with these tabs on the loudspeaker. Caution: Do not
destroy the loudspeaker membrane! You will immediately recognize
which way you have to insert the cardboard when you look at the
environment of the loudspeaker. Try then the same sound output
without and with this device and you will recognize a tremendous
difference in quality und loudness.
