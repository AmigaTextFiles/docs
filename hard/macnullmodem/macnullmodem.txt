This archive contains details of how to build a null modem cable to
connect an Apple Mac and Amiga/PC together via their serial ports.

The document has been converted to a PDF file for distribution. In order
to read PDF files you need Adobe Acrobat Reader or a similar PDF viewer.

Although not a particularly 'Amiga friendly' format, due to the nature
of the document it is assumed that you have access to either a Mac or PC
compatible. For those who don't I have converted the document into GIF
images. An archive containing these can be downloaded from here;

  http://www.dynamix.force9.co.uk/files/mac_nullmodem_gif.lha

If you need any further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact me.
