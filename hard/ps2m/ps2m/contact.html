This project was done by Denis Sotchenko, well known ;) as Russian Digital Computing (RDC). Some words about me: I was born in 1975 year, now live in Moscow, my hobbies are electronics, computers, internet, books and films (science fiction and phantasy). My first computer (i8080 based) I soldered in 1988. Now I am a programmer-professional in embedded applications (control, communications, security, home electonics). This project was done because of my experience in these areas.
