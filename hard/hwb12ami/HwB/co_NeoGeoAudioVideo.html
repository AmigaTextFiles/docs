NeoGeo Audio/Video

Availble on the NeoGeo videogame.

(At the Computer)

8 PIN DIN (DIN45326) FEMALE at the Computer.

Pin Name Dir Description
1 AOUT Audio out
2 GND Ground
3 VIDEO Composite Video Out
4 +5V +5 VDC
5 GREEN Green Video
6 RED Red Video
7 NSYNC Negative Sync
8 BLUE Blue Video
Note: Direction is Computer relative Monitor.
