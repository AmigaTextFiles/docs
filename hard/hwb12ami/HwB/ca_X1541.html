X1541 Cable

Used to transfer data from a Commodore 1541/1581 diskdrive to a PC. The X1541 software is written by Leopoldo Ghielmetti.

(To the PC).
(To the Diskdrive)

25 PIN D-SUB MALE to the PC.
6 PIN DIN (DIN45322) MALE to the Cable

PC Diskdrive
GND 18-25 2 GND
STROBE 1 3 ATN
AUTOFEED 14 4 CLOCK
SELECTIN 17 5 DATA
INIT 16 6 RESET
