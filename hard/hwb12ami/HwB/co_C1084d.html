Commodore 1084 & 1084S (Digital)

(At the Monitor)

8 PIN DIN 'C' FEMALE at the Monitor.

Pin Name Description
1 n/c Not connected
2 R Red
3 G Green
4 B Blue
5 I Intensity
6 GND Ground
7 HSYNC Horizontal Sync
8 VSYNC Vertical Sync
