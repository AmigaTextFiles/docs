Videotype: Analogue.

(At the videocard)
(At the monitor cable)

9 PIN D-SUB FEMALE at the videocard.
9 PIN D-SUB MALE at the monitor cable.

Pin Name Description
1 R Red
2 G Green
3 B Blue
4 CSYNC Composite Sync
5 MODE Mode Control
6 RGND Red Ground
7 GGND Green Ground
8 BGND Blue Ground
9 GND Ground
