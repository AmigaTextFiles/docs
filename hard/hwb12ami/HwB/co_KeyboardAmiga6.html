Keyboard (6 Amiga)

(At the computer)

6 PIN MINI-DIN FEMALE (PS/2 STYLE) (A4000/CD32/CDTV) at the computer.

Pin Name Dir Description
1 /DATA Data
2 n/c - Not connected
3 GND Ground
4 +5V +5 Volts DC (100 mA max)
5 CLOCK Clock
6 n/c - Not connected
Note: Direction is Computer relative Keyboard.
