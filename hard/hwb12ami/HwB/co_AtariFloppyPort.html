Atari Floppy Port

(At the Computer)
(At the Diskdrive)

14 PIN DIN FEMALE at the Computer.
14 PIN DIN MALE at the Diskdrive.

Pin Name Description
1 RD Read Data
2 SIDE0 Side 0 Select
3 GND Ground
4 INDEX Index
5 SEL0 Drive 0 Select
6 SEL1 Drive 1 Select
7 GND Ground
8 MOTOR Motor On
9 DIR Direction In
10 STEP Step
11 WD Write Data
12 WG Write Gate
13 TRK00 Track 00
14 WP Write Protect
