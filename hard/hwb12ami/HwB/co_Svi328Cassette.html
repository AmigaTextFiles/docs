(At the computer)

7 PIN FEMALE EDGE CONNECTOR at the computer.

Pin Name Description
1 12v Power 100mA
2 CASR Cassette data read
3 CASW Cassette data write
4 AUDIO Cassette audio
5 GND System ground
6 ME  
7 READY System Ready
