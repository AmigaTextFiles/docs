MIDI In

MIDI=Musical Instrument Digital Interface.

(At the peripheral)
(At the cable)

5 PIN DIN 180° (DIN41524) FEMALE at the peripheral.
5 PIN DIN 180° (DIN41524) MALE at the cable.

Pin Name Description
1 n/c Not connected
2 n/c Not connected
3 n/c Not connected
4 CSRC Current Source
5 CSINK Current Sink
