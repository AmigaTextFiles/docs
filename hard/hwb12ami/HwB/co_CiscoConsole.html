Cisco Console Port

Used to configure a Cisco router.

(At the Cisco hub)
(At the cables)

RJ45 FEMALE CONNECTOR at the Cisco routers.
RJ45 MALE CONNECTOR at the cables.

2 DTR Data Terminal Ready
