Amstrad Digital Joystick

Availble at the Amstrad CPC6128 and CPC6128 Plus.

(At the Computer)
(At the Joystick cable)

9 PIN D-SUB MALE at the Computer.
9 PIN D-SUB FEMALE at the Joystick cable.

Digital Joystick 1

Pin Name Dir Description
1 UP Up
2 DOWN Down
3 LEFT Left
4 RIGHT Right
5 n/c - Not connected
6 FIRE2 Fire button 2
7 FIRE1 Fire button 1
8 GND Ground
9 GND Ground

Digital Joystick 2

Pin Name Dir Description
1 UP Up
2 DOWN Down
3 LEFT Left
4 RIGHT Right
5 n/c - Not connected
6 FIRE2 Fire button 2
7 FIRE1 Fire button 1
8 GND Ground
9 n/c - Not connected
Note: Direction is Computer relative Joystick.
