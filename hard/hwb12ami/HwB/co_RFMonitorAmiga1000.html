Amiga 1000 RF Monitor

(At the computer)

8 PIN DIN "C" FEMALE at the computer.

Pin Name Dir Description
1 n/c - Not connected
2 GND Ground
3 AUDL Audio Left
4 CVIDEO Composite Video
5 GND Ground
6 n/c - Not connected
7 +12V +12 VDC
8 AUDR Audio Right
Note: Direction is Computer relative Monitor.
