C128 RGBI

(At the Computer)

9 PIN D-SUB FEMALE at the Computer.

Pin Name Dir Description
1 GND Ground
2 GND Ground
3 R Red
4 G Green
5 B Blue
6 I Intensity
7 VIDEO Composite Video
8 HSYNC Horizontal Sync
9 VSYNC Vertical Sync
Note: Direction is Computer relative Monitor.
