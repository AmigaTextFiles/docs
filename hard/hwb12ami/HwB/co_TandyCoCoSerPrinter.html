CoCo Serial Printer

Availble on the Tandy Color Computer, also known as CoCo.

(At the computer)

4 PIN DIN 270° FEMALE at the computer.

Pin Name Description
1 NC  
2 /BUSY Enabled when the printer is busy
3 GND  
4 DATA RS-232 level data
