CBM 1902A

Availble on the Commodore CBM 1902A monitor.

(At the Monitor)

6 PIN DIN FEMALE at the Monitor.

Pin Name Dir Description
1 n/c - Not connected
2 AUDIO Audio
3 GND Ground
4 C Chroma
5 n/c - Not connected
6 L Luminance
Note: Direction is Monitor relative Computer.
