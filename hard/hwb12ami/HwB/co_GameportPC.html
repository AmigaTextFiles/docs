PC Gameport

(At the computer)
(At the joystick cable)

15 PIN D-SUB FEMALE at the computer.
15 PIN D-SUB MALE at the joystick cable.

Pin Name Dir Description
1 +5V +5 VDC
2 /B1 Button 1
3 X1 Joystick 1 - X
4 GND Ground
5 GND Ground
6 Y1 Joystick 1 - Y
7 /B2 Button 2
8 +5V +5 VDC
9 +5V +5 VDC
10 /B4 Button 4
11 X2 Joystick 2 - X
12 GND Ground
13 Y2 Joystick 2 - Y
14 /B3 Button 3
15 +5V +5 VDC
Note: Direction is Computer relative Joystick.
Note: Use 100kohm resistor.
