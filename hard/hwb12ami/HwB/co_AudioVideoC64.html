5 PIN DIN 180° (DIN41524) MALE at the Cable.

Pin Name Dir Description
1 LUM Luminance
2 GND Ground
3 AOUT Audio Out
4 VOUT Video Out
5 AIN Audio In
Note: Direction is Computer relative Monitor.
