Music

This drawer includes a selection of mods and other audio files taken from various sources.
This section is officially realized and mantained by Fabio "FBY" Barzagli (mods and other 8-bit audio files) and by Vanni "Liulai" Torelli (16-bit audio files).
The featured mods are usually included in the .MOD format or in the .MED one. They can be easily played by configuring the module player in the ARPrefs tool.
