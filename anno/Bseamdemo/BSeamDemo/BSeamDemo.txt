What is BSeamDemo?

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

BSeamDemo is a demonstration version of Black Seam Seamless Textures Volume
One, a collection of two-hundred seamless texture maps for use with Lightwave 3D, 3D 
Studio, or any other 3D modeling and animation system. The textures can also be used for
Web page design, or any other application where seamless textures can be used.
Black Seam offers a variety of texture surfaces, in true 24 bit color, and is a worthwhile 
product for anyone who is involved in generating 3D images. The full version of the software is 
available for $45.00 plus shipping and handling, and it is available in the following file 
formats:  Amiga IFF, Targa and SGI. It will be shipped on an ISO9660 compatable CDROM.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

About the Demonstration Version and the Full Version:

The demonstration version of Black Seam textures is limited to 30 images, at 256X256
resolution, in 128 colors. The full version features 200 images, at 256X256 resolution, 
in full 24-bit color. For further information regarding ordering the full version of Black 
Seam Volume One, please refer to the address and phone number at the end of this 
text.
