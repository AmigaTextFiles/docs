Commodore Power

(At the Powersupply cable)

This Connetor are at C= Keyboard-Computer.
first at C128, then at A500(+), A600 and A1200;
also at CDTV and CD32? Powersupply cables.

Pin Name Color in cable Description Ampere PC std. Color
1 +12V   Brown +12VDC 0.5 A   Yellow
2 GND   Black Ground -   Black
3 -12V   White -12 VDC 0.1A   Blue
4 GND   Yellow Shield GND -   Black
5 +5V   Red +5 VDC 3A or 4.5A   Red
Note: Don't know if "Color in cable" is always the same!
 Please check the real Voltage before first connect.
The -5V Supply is not supported, the Computer use -12V instad.
