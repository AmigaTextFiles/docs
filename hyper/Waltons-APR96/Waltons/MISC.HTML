RANTS :

Well, there are some people you believe, and are those you trust, and those that just ain't worth asking. A while back, when my BBS was running, I was 'reliably' informed that my SCSI card would NOT run on the 4000. It would just about run a tape streamer I was told, but anything else ... nope not a chance. Well here I am, a Amiga 4000 with that same SCSI card, with a DAT drive, a 520MB Hard Drive and a Toshiba CD-Rom, all working off that same SCSI card that would only run a tape drive (if I was lucky). So I ask you, what is the best advice to take?? I'd say that you take your own advice and sod every one else! Experts - who needs them!
