Terrorvision!!

So just who are Terrorvison ?


The band from Bradford, England.........

Terrorvision have to be one of the biggest bands in England at the moment. They formed the band in Bradford, England and were originally called 'Spoilt Bratz'. Soon after they changed the name to 'Terrorvision' and were signed by top record label EMI. They made their first album in December 1992, entitled 'Formaldehyde' After this was released, top British Rock magazine, 'Kerranngg', gave them a lot of stick about it and constantly put down the band. A few years later in 1994, they brought out there next album, which was an instant hit. It included songs like Middleman, Oblivion and Alice Whats the Matter. This album was entitled 'How to Make Friends and Influence People'. From here on, Kerrannggg seemed to love them. They were and still are in vertually every issuse of the magazine and made regular appearances on the
front covers (see picture). After winning a few awards, they then bought out their newest album, 'Regular Urban Survivors'his album was also an instant classic.

The music


Terrorvision's music is very different to that of any other band. They have their own unique style, that mixes catchy rythm and a solid beat. Each song is a classic, and they all have that little touch to them that no other band has. In mine, and many other people's opinion the best thing about their music, is that it is so varied. One minute you can be head banging to a heavy rock song like 'Didnt Bleed Red', the next minute you can chilling out to the melow tones of 'Ten Shades of Grey'. They have a great mixture of heavy rock, light rock, middle of the road and even easy listening. If you take a song like 'Pretend Best Friend', you will find that it even has a great jazz sound. Over all...Terrorvision are a great band to follow and listen to. Go check them out NOW!!!!!!!!
