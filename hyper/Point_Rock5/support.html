How to contribute to the next edition of Point & Rock


You can feel free to send me :


  • Articles

    You can write some articles for me about whatever you want :
    • Your likes and dislikes about an album
    • A concert report
    • News and rumours about bands, albums, dates and so on
    • Your favourite band

    If you write an article, it can be sent to me in ASCII format, or preferably already in HTML ( I'm a lazzy boy you know... ) with pictures, backgrounds... In case of ASCII format, try to send me too some pictures to add to the text if you have any.


  • Concert Dates

    I will draw a table of concert dates for every country as far as I receive datas.


  • Digitized pictures of albums'covers


  • Digitized pictures of bands


  • Title Pictures

    You are a skilled graphician and want to draw a good title picture for the mag ??? You use 3D rendering every day ??? Just show me your work and the best picture will be the title picture of the next issue !!!


  • Adverts

    You want to publish an advert to promote your band, swap material or stuff ( music stuff ), seek ( and destroy ? ) some HEAVY contacts or anything else ? Just send me your advert. It will be published in the next edition of Point & Rock.


All this stuff must be sent to the Point & Rock Headquarters


Everyone who send me support stuff will have his name in the greetings.
