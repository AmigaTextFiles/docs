Graspop Metal Meeting 97 : 29 june


This memorable sunday, we were travelling ( Ace and I ) to Dessel to assist this Metal festival. There was some good bands playing there and we hoped to have a nice day. The weather was not so bright but we have the habit here in Belgium ( even in summer vacation ! ).

When we arrived, the first thing was to find a place to park the car and we found it in a field ( which became later a horrible battle field ( see further ) ).

We came just in time to see Uncle Meat. In fact, we were on the festival ground during their first song. It's the band of a friend and it was the second time I saw them.
Despite some technical problems ( this time it was not the drum kit ;-) ), they have received a better welcome than the first time I saw them at the Kiss' show in Gent.

After their gig, we went to the "market tents" to see if something interesting was to sell. I found there the tour book of Iron Maiden at the Monsters of Rock 92 in Castle Donington. Entombed was playing on the main stage. Their show seemed not so bad but I have not seen them. And that's here that Mrs Rain came.

Then it was the time of Samael to play but I have not seen a lot 'coz it was raining and we went to the marquee stage ( under a giant tent ) to see the first band playing there : Dimmu Borgir. One thing to say about those Black Metal bands is that I prefer to listen this kind of dark and atmospheric music in my sleeping room than live ( but their shows are nice to see ).

At 12:30, it was time to meet Uncle Meat at their signature session ( and time for me to meet my IRC contact Afreak, the drummer of the Uncle ( hi mate ! ) ). It has been the occasion to talk with the band ( their bassist speaks well french !!! ) and to come back with their signed albums.

Moving to the main stage to see Grip Inc. Wow ! What a gig... This band is fantastic and Dave Lombardo is really a great drummer !!! Also the singer who is a good frontman, always speaking to the crowd to excite it.

Back to the Marquee ( we've walked a lot this day !! ) to see The Gathering. It was a pure beauty. The singer ( a woman ) has a so wonderful voice, contrasting with the guitars in the back. Their music is quite slow and depressive, but it's superb. The Gathering are one of the best bands I've seen at this festival.

Saxon played on the main stage under a continuous rain. I liked their Heavy Metal ( back to the good old days ). The funniest thing at their gig was the mud-battle between fans in the crowd. The singer asked to stop to throw mud on stage or the band would not finish their show, but no one listend to him, the battle was raging on !!!

Back to the... guess what ??? The Marquee !!! It was to see Cradle of Filth. It's just the same note than for Dimmu Borgir, but the light show was impressive. The singer of this band is astonishing. He can sing with a very deep voice and 2 seconds later, he's in the high notes !!! Great...

After it, we went away to visit the place. There was a third stage : the Skate stage. We just saw Deviate there. Having seen them 3 weeks ago, we didn't stayed there a lot. We went to the Metal Market. You can see the Skate Stage and the Metal Market on this picture ( respectively left and right ).

As you can see on this photograph, the rain has already made a lot of damages to the ground. It was a giant mud-bath...
Inside the Metal Market, water was everywhere and I wonder yet now how the merchandising has not been damaged....

I have not found something to buy there but my friend bought the last album of The Gathering and a bootleg of his favourite band : Kiss.

Here you have a view of the main stage with Biohazard playing ( you can see a part of their last album logo at the extreme right of the picture ). I have not seen them because I was speaking with Afreak ( I met him randomly as I was coming back from the Metal Market ). The keyboard player of Uncle Meat was there also, so we've drank a beer while speaking about....computers ! Afreak and I have an Amiga and the other has a Macintosh... Not really rock and roll, but it's another passion !

We took some pictures ( you can see a picture of Afreak and I in the "Last Words" section ) and waited the coming of Moonspell in the Marquee. This gig was good but I don't remember a lot about this band... Sorry !

Time to see the Megadeth on the Main stage. I've took some good pictures there of the band. You can see here Dave Mustaine who was in great form !!! The band gave us a very good gig with some classics. The new compositions are well rendered live but we had to wait a long time to hear the first extract from "Cryptic Writings". They've played "Trust", "Almost Honest", "Use The Man" and maybe one or two others I don't remember. A great moment was when they played "A tout le monde" : everybody was singing with Dave. Great !!!

Just one note ( but I don't have a scan to show it ), David Ellefson has cut his hair. It seems that all Hard Rockers are bored with their long hairs....










At the end of the show, I went to the food-stands to buy something to eat ( it was 9 pm ). I came back to the Marquee with fried potatoes !!! My Dying Bride was canceled and we saw Tiamat instead. I saw them last year at the same festival, but it was during the day with no light-show and it was not so great. This year, it's radically different ! Their depressive and strange music was supported with an impressive light-show and all was marvelous. It's another band that pleased me this year. Unfortunately, I have not seen the entire show because we wanted to be near the main stage to see the last band : Alice Cooper.

Alice was one of the "old artist" I wanted to see, it's done now !!! And I must say that even at 50+ years old, this guy is yet great !!! Damn, he has a great presence on stage ( but sometimes he disappears during 5 minutes backstage to change his clothes while the musicians are playing ). All the eras of Alice has been covered. From "The Last Temptation" ( his last real album ) to "School's Out". It has been a real pleasure to hear some classics such "I'm 18" ( especially for me, my nick is Nr_18 !!! ), "School's out", "Poison", "No More Mr Nice Guy", "Billion Dollars Babies"...

There was also a visual show. At a moment, some doctors are coming to catch the "gone mad" Alice and put him in a straight jacket. After some minutes, Alice get himself free and killed the doctors. I liked it...

The show finished with "School's Out" ( and for me, school's out for ever !!! ). It's with a great smile on our faces that we left the festival ground.




This smile was soon deleted by the bad surprise we had when we came back to the parking lot ! The first lucky guys who left the parking before the others had damaged the field. I explain : with the continuous rain, this field has been transformed in a mega mud-battle-field. So, everyone has been caught in this trap. All cars were blocked and personaly, we have been blocked until 10:00 am the morning after !!! 10 hours in a car, waiting a rescue... Damn !!! It has been long.

Despite it, I find that this festival has been one of the best I've made !!!

See you next year !!!
