Channel Zero : Black Fuel





For non-belgian readers, Channel Zero is a belgian metal band coming from the region of Brussels and it's a band we can be proud of !!! Their last album ( the fourth ) is on sale since some months now but I've had not yet the occasion to test it.

The first time you listen to the album, you could say "yes, it's a good piece of work", but no more. And the more you hear the CD, the more you enter in. In fact, this album needs more than a single audition to be fully understood.

I've had the occasion to talk to the band recently and I told it to them. They replied me that I was not the first person to say it. I think it's a sign of maturation !

The first track of the album ( "Black Fuel" ) is very effective. It has a simplistic guitar riff but it kills !!! I understand why they decided to title this album with this song.
I even think that "Love/Hate Satellite" could easily figure on Slayer's "Undisputed Attitude" ( the singer has the same tone than Araya in this song ).

"Black Fuel" is quite diversified and I like when it is. But I tell you again to listen it more than 1 time to fully appreciate it...

Channel Zero begins to get known outside Belgium, so I think that you should have not so many difficulties to find the album in Europe.

My quote : 9/10
