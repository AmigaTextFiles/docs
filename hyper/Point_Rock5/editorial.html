Heads up !!!



Finaly, I'm back on the track with the fifth issue of your favourite magazine : Point and Rock. Nearly 1 year after the first issue, I am very proud now to show you the BIG improvements made to the mag.

As you can see, it has grown ( the size is bigger than previous issues ), but most important, the format has changed.

A lot of readers asked me to write the mag in HTML, but I didn't knew how to do it until I wrote the code for my Homepage. It's now a fact, Point & Rock will see the light of day under HTML format. It allows to create a magazine with a lot of graphics, pictures, making it nicer to read.

And if you say HTML, you think Internet... So I went to geocities to take a place where I could store Point & Rock. Now, not only Amiga users are allowed to read me, but also PC and Mac, thus the ENTIRE world. What an enhancement !!

I really hope that this new magazine will please you. I let you discover the changes and I hope to receive A LOT of reactions from you, dear readers.

In this issue, you will read concert reports from the gig of Kiss in Gent ( Belgium ), the Graspop Metal "Mud" Meeting in Dessel ( Belgium ), the usual news, concert dates and album tests, of course the charts ( from this issue, only albums are classed ).

Now, welcome to my nightmare ( this fifth issue has been hard to be done ), have a nice reading...

Let's ROCK !!!
