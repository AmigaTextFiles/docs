Q: Is there any good strategy?
A: Every player develop one.

Pay attention that if you dig upward and place yourself over a falling
apple, enemies coming from bottom will tend to allineate with your X axis.

When you take the cake at the center of the screen enemies will stop,
you can easily kill them while the AplhaMonsters are descending.

If you miss only one letter to make "EXTRA" you can wait to reach a very
nasty level then take it here and finish it in a flash without losing lives.
