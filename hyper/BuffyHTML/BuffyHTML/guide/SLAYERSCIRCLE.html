Slayer's Circle

IMPORTANT NOTICE

I found out recently to my absolute horror (really!) that the Slayer's Circle letters I had so far were only part of volume 4, and three other volumes existed! It's tedious enough just to reformat 10 of these letters in a row (there's more to do than you'd think) never mind about 256 of the damn things!

Thinking about this and the fact that the information is usually out of date by the time it's uploaded to Aminet, made me realise how very little I actually use Slayer's Circle, and it makes no sense to keep it if no-one uses it. So if you use it, email me and tell me. Otherwise I'm scrapping it. Slayer's Circle will be deleted entirely if no emails are received by lunchtime on Friday December 1st, 2000. Put into your subject line, something like 'KEEP SLAYER'S CIRCLE' to make sure I read it straight away. Sorry if you think this is a bit harsh. Some people are too afraid to remove redundant features from their guides or programs. Not me, dude.

You need not feel guilt for not using this section. I want to know how many people use it, not how many people think it would be a shame if it goes. But anyway for now, here's the Slayer's Circle newsletters.

Remember, you can always sign up to receive this letter at http://www.buffyslayer.com.
