
COMMODORE (R) ANNOUNCES AMIGA 3000 (R) WITH SCALA (R)
PACKAGE AT OUTSTANDING NEW PRICE OF $1499*

(West Chester, PA --- September 24, 1992) --- Commodore Business
Machines, Inc. today announced that its Amiga (R) 3000 Presentation
System, bundled with the new SCALA (R)  Multimedia 200 software, is
now available in the U.S. through the end of November* at the
outstanding promotional price of $1499. Prior to this promotion, the
package had a suggested retail price of over $3100*. Now, promotion
savings range from $1725 to $2015*, depending on the system purchased.

The Amiga 3000 Presentation System along with the SCALA Multimedia
200 software provides a dynamic, easy-to-use, professional package that
enables users to create spectacular graphic presentations. The two
presentation system offerings are: the A3000-25/50 system at the
promotional price of $1499*; and the A3000-25/100 system at the
promotional price of $1859*. Both systems offer the full capabilities of
the Amiga 3000.
