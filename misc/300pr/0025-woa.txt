World of Commodore/Amiga will be held April 24-26 at the New York
City Passenger Ship Terminal, Pier 88 (between 48th & 52nd on the
Hudson River).

The show will be open Friday, Saturday and Sunday from 10 AM - 5 PM.

Admission is $15 each day or $30 for a 3-day pass. (Seminars are 
included in the price of admission.)
