$500 REBATE ON AMIGA 3000-25 SERIES COMPUTERS ENDS SOON

Commodore's $500 cash rebate on Amiga 3000-25 series computers is
scheduled to end on April 30, 1992. The rebate program has been
very successful. Demand for professional Amiga products has been
very strong throughout the promotion. The products eligible for the
rebate are:

	Amiga 3000-25/50
	Amiga 3000-25/100
	Amiga 3000T-25/200

The offer is valid on purchases made from participating Authorized
Commodore Resellers in the U.S. The rebate also applies to purchases
made under the education and government programs. 

Authorized Commodore Resellers are also featuring bundle specials on
Amiga 2000 and 3000 series computers.

To locate an Authorized Commodore Reseller in your area, call 
(800) 66-AMIGA.
