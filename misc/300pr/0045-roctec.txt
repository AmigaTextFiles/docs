                 HURRICANE ANDREW RELIEF FUND CONTRIBUTION

Hurricane Andrew which swept through the southern part of the US left
it's toll on hundreds of thousands of people who are now in desperate
need.  As a means of showing our support for the victims we are
asking you to join us and and help them out with a financial contribution.

Effective through Friday, September 4, 1992, a portion of every order
will go to relief funds for the Andrew victims.

The chart below shows how much from each product will be donated.
