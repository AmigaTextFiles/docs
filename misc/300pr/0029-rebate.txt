
	COMMODORE ANNOUNCES EXTENSION OF $500 REBATE ON
		AMIGA (R) 3000-25 SERIES PRODUCTS

(West Chester, PA -- May 4, 1992) -- Commodore Business Machines, Inc.
(U.S.) announced the extension of the $500 Cash Rebate on Amiga 3000
25 MHz products today. Citing strong response from customers and 
Authorized Resellers, Commodore will extend the rebate offer through
May 31, 1992. The $500 Cash Rebate is offered on the Amiga 3000-25/50,
3000-25/100 and the Amiga 3000T-25/200 systems. The Cash Rebate can
be used at the time of purchase or customers will receive a check
directly from Commodore (at the dealer's discretion).

In addition, Commodore announced today that its Authorized Resellers
are offering discounts of up to $1800 off selected Amiga systems.
For more information on these discounts, contact your local Commodore
Authorized Reseller. To locate your nearest Commodore Authorized
Reseller, call (800) 66-AMIGA.
