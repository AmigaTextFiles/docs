	H & M PUBLICATIONS ANNOUNCES AMIGA.INFO SUBSCRIPTION RATES

(SEABROOK, TX, FEBRUARY 6, 1993) - H & M Publications announces
subscription rates for Amiga.info magazine.

Amiga.info direct mail subscription rates for U.S. Domestic will be
$10.00 for six issues. Foreign subscribers should add an additional
$5.00. The magazine will be published bi-monthly (six times annually).

Subscriptions can be ordered from H & M Publications at the address
below. Orders must be accompanied by either check or money order. We
currently do not accept credit card orders. Make checks or money orders
payable to H & M Publications. Payments must be made in U.S. Funds.

Amiga.info will carry timely reviews and information on such topics as:
Multimedia, Graphics, Audio, Video, New Products, Technical Support (C and
C++ programming, etc.), Telecommunications (latest and greatest PD and
information about networks and BBSes) and in depth reviews of hardware,
software and games. We are also open to suggestions! Make this magazine
what you would like it to be by submitting articles for our consideration.
