	H & M PUBLICATIONS SEEKS AMIGA WRITERS AND COLUMNISTS

(SEABROOK, TX, FEBRUARY 6, 1993) - H & M Publications is looking for Amiga
writers and columnists for it's new Amiga magazine Amiga.info. If you have
a program or article that you would like to submit to us for review please
send it to:

			Amiga.info magazine
			H & M Publications
			Attn: Submissions Editor
			P.O. Box 833
			Seabrook, Texas 77586

Articles should include your name, address, phone number and social
security number. Requests for our Writer's Guidelines should be sent to
the address above along with a self-addressed business sized envelope.

H & M Publications is not obligated to return unsolicited materials. If you
would like your materials returned you must include a self-addressed
stamped mailer or envelope. H & M Publications does not accept unsolicited
product reviews.

H & M Publications is also looking for columnists from a variety of Amiga
user backgrounds. If you specialize in a certain Amiga application (such as
graphics, video, music, programming, productivity, etc.) please send us
information about your abilities. This information should include a resume,
past work history, and examples of previously published material.

For advertising or subscription information please contact David Martin or
Trent Havard. You can also contact us via EMAIL on USENET at
davidm@cup.portal.com or 72510,3232 on Compuserve.
