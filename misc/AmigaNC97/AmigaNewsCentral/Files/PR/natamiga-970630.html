                    G R E G   A N D   J O E   B I T S 
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Well, the OpenHouse was a success. We had around 150 people come out to
  see what we had going on. 
  Fleecy Moss was kind enough to do a write up on the openhouse.
  It's available at www.jms.org/news0002.htm

* The online catalogue has finally been updated. It's still not perfect yet,
  but MANY of the prices have been updated, and unavailable items have been
  removed.

* There is a possibility of 3.1 kits in the very very near future. If you
  were looking for one, please place the order now to make sure you get one!

* Well, PowerPC cards did not make it for the OpenHouse. Those were pushed
  back again by phase5

* A demo of AWEB-3 is now available on Aminet. Several new features including
  frames! Well worth looking at.

* Our ISP has been kind of buggered lately. We've been operating at 64k as
  opposed to 128k for the last week and sometimes it hangs up. They say
  they are working on it, albeit slowly..

* Now that all popular browsers for the Amiga support frames, we'll be
  implementing some changes to our web-site in the near future.

* Since distribution for Amiga products in North America has gone into the
  crapper, we are looking to start buying products directly from developers.

  If you are a developer and are having a hard time getting your products
  into North America, please contact me directly at, gscott@nationalamiga.com
