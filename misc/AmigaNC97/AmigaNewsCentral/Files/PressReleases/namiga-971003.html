                    G R E G   A N D   J O E   B I T S 
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
* What's Intel going to do when they run out of disco songs? It's bad
  enough they are trying to dominate the processor market, now they are
  trying to re-invent the 70's.

* Can anyone shed some light as to why Haage and Partner and Phase5 just
  don't want to co-operate anymore?

* Amiga Inc is definately moving forward. They held interviews for the
  last few weeks in SD. And they ARE hiring. Trust me :)

* Legacy magazine is the new all-on-video magazine produced by the
  Amiga Report staff. We will have it available for sale for $24CAD.
  It includes up-to-date news items in the Amiga community, as well
  as tutorials and inside track. It's on its way to 

* nationalamiga.com will be taking on some major changes in about 1 week.
  Newer, faster, better.. Even the catalogue will be tied into our inventory
  database so you can get realtime updates as to what we actually have.

* Greg will be attending the MidWest Amiga Exposition. Look for the guy
  in the National Amiga T-Shirt!

* Insert 104's are delayed for the time being. I'm not sure when we'll be
  able to get anymore at this time.

* We are now distributing CD-Solutions monitors for Amiga computers.
  Check out the specs and pricing below! Dealer enquiries welcome!

* It appears that an Amiga show will happen this winter.  The show will be 
  held in Toronto, Ont. Canada, the beginning of November.
  More details to follow.

* I have discovered some things about Amiga Inc. They will be taking
  the direction that another company has already tried. You will see
  what I mean in the very near future.

* Canada Post parcel rates go up starting October 1st. So there will be a
  slight increase in shipping charges for this method.

* Just to note, all policies and such can be found right on the orderform
  itself. www.nationalamiga.com/ordering.html

* Drew Carrey's new show opening "Cleavland Rocks!" is damn cool.

* Phase5 has discontinued all 040 products due to lack of 040 CPUs.

* Phase5 has discontinued the MkII line of CPU cards. Soon they will put
  out the MkIII's, which are basically the PPC cards, but without the PPC
  CPU. They will NOT be upgradable though.

* We have just too many C64's sitting around here. So we want to get rid of
  them. Now here's the deal.. You order over $200 worth of stuff, and we'll
  give you a Commodore 64 computer for free! Yes FREE! As long as you know
  it will add to the shipping weight..  While supplies last!

* AmigaZone - the COMPLETE Amiga online service. Text interface.
  Web Interface. $19.95USD/month, no limits. FREE 2-week trials.
  www.amigazone.com for details!
