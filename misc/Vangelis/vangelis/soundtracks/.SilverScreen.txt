                        Vangelis On The Silver Screen

                                     ***


Vangelis has composed the score for many motion pictures.  Though many of his
projects  have  been  with  Ridley Scott and Frederic Rossif, his talents are
sought all across the world.

His filmography is arranged for you in chronological order. 

The 70s

      1970: Sex Power - score composer
      1972: Salut, Jerusalem - score composer
      1972: L'Apocalypse Des Animaux - score composer
      1973: Amore - score composer
      1973: Crime and Passion - score composer
      1975: No Oyes Ladrar los Perros? - score composer
      1976: La Fete Sauvage - score composer
      1977: Entends-Tu les Chiens Aboyer - score composer

The 80s

      1981: Chariots of Fire - score composer, music arrangement, music
      1981: Die Todesgottin des Liebescamps - assistant camera operator
      1982: Blade Runner - score composer, music, music arrangement
      1982: Missing - score composer, music arrangement
      1982: Pablo Picasso - score composer
      1983: Antarctica - score composer
      1983: Wonders of Life - score composer
      1983: The Year of Living Dangerously - score composer
      1984: The Bounty - score composer, music
      1984: Sauvage et Beau - score composer
      1987: Nosferatu a Venezia - score composer
      1987: Someone to Watch Over Me - song composer
      1988: Le Diner des Bustes (short) - score composer
      1988: De Nuremburg A Nuremberg - score composer
      1989: Francesco - score composer
      1989: Russicum - song composer

The 90s

      1992: 1492: Conquest of Paradise - music
      1992: Bitter Moon/Lune de Fiel - music
