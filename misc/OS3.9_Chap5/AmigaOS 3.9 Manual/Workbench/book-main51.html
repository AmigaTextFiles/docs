Printer Editor

The Printer editor lets you specify the type of printer you are using, and the type of output you want. The Printer Preferences editor is described in detail in Chapter 9.

PrinterGfx Editor

The PrinterGfx editor supports extended printer graphics features. For example, PrinterGfx lets you determine your margins, select and correct the colors, set the dithering method, and scale your graphics. The PrinterGfx Preferences editor is described in Chapter 9.
