Amiga Arena Interview
---------------------

with Sascha Brandt of Ancor!
http://www.ancor.ch


*Please intorduce yourself to the readers!

To put it shortly, I'm a freak.

*When did you start working with the Amiga?

I think it must have been about 1991

*Which Amiga models do you own?

A4000T PPC233/060
A2000
A500
CD32

*Which version of the OS is running on your computer?

I think you're referring to my A4000T, that would be OS 3.9

*Which software are you developing?

I'm not developing the software, I'm only responsible for the graphics, the sound, the direction and the story.

*Can you tell us what your software is about?

It is an adventure called "The last seal", which - as usual - is about saving mankind. There will also be a couple of action sequences for a change, where you can kick some malignant and disgusting creatures' a...

*How long has development taken so far?

Hmmm... about 3 years.

It's just a game.

Unfortunately it's not finished yet.
