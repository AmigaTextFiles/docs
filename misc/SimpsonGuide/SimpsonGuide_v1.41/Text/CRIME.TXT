TESTIMONY
about Events at the Crime Scene

Dennis Fung           LAPD Criminalist. Testified about the
                      collection of evidence at the Bundy
                      crime scene and at Simpson´s estate.

Ronald Phillips       LAPD Homicide Coordinator. Testified
                      that he and Mark Fuhrman arrived at
                      the crime scene together. He also
                      told the jury that he was the one who
                      called O. J. in Chicago and told him
                      of the killings. He said O. J. did
                      not ask him when, where or how Nicole
                      was killed.

Robert Riske          LAPD Officer. First Officer at the
                      murder scene. Testified that moments
                      after he called for an ambulance he
                      realized that both victims where
                      dead. He then went inside and used
                      Nicole´s phone. He also testified
                      that he saw only one glove.

David Rossi           LAPD Officer. Testified that no one
                      walked through the blood at the crime
                      scene while he was there.
