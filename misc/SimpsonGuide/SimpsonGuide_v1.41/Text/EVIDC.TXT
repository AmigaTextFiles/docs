TESTIMONY
on Evidence Found at the Crimescene

William Bodziak       Special Agent with the FBI. He
                      testified that bloody shoe prints
                      leading away from the two bodies were
                      left by a pair of $ 160 "Bruo Magli"
                      shoes, size 12. The Italian designer
                      shoes are the same size as Simpson´s.

Doug Deedrick         FBI hair and fibre expert. Testified
                      that hair similar to Ronald Goldman´s
                      was found on the right-hand glove,
                      discovered at Simpson´s  estate.
                      He also stated that hair similar to
                      that of O. J. Simpson was found on
                      Ronald Goldman´s shirt.

Dr. Lakshmanan        Los Angeles County Coroner. Testified
                      that the wounds on the victims´
                      bodies could have been caused by one
                      knife and that the murders could have
                      been committed in a very short peroid
                      of time.
