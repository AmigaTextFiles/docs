TESTIMONY
On When the Murders Took Place

Ellen Aaronson.       Brentwood resident. Had a blind date
                      with Danny Mandel that evening.

Francesca Harman      She testified that she didn´t hear
                      any anusual noise when she drove by
                      the crimescene around 10.20 pm.

Robert Heidstra       Brentwood resident. Testified that
                      at 10.40 pm he heard two voices. He
                      said that what he heard sounded like
                      an argument, during which one of the
                      two persons yelled "Hey, hey, hey!"
                      Shortly afterwards he heared a gate
                      slam. He said he couldn´t understand
                      what the men were saying because of
                      a dog barking.
                      But he also testified that he saw a
                      white car, possibly a Ford Bronco,
                      leave the crimescene.

Danny Mandel          Had a blind date with Ellen Aaronson.
                      They both testified that after they
                      had left the restaurant around 10 pm
                      they walked down South Bundy. They
                      said they didn´t notice anything
                      unusual when they came across the
                      crimescene and didn´t see or hear a
                      barking dog.

Denise Pilnak         Former neighbor of Nicole. Testified
                      she didn´t hear anything unusual when
                      she walked a friend to her car at
                      10.18 pm. But at 10.35, when she went
                      into her bathroom, she heared a dog
                      barking. The dog was still barking
                      when she went to bed at 12.30 pm.
