Frequently Requested Amiga Fonts

  1. First place to look for fonts is the AMINET archive. This is the biggest archive of Amiga software and there you will find also quite a lot of fonts. The Aminet consists of many mirrors around the world. Here are some of them:

    @enumerate @item ftp.wustl.edu, @item ftp.luth.se, @item ftp.eunet.ch, @item ftp.uni-paderborn.de, @item ftp.doc.ic.ac.uk. @end enumerate

    Just log in as ftp and go to the directory

    /pub/aminet/text/font.

  2. Another good ftp server to look is the CICA-server:

    @enumerate

    @item ftp.cica.indiana.edu

    @end enumerate

    To this server are also some mirrors around the world available.

  3. Also a good place to look for is the following WWW server:

    @enumerate

    @item http://jasper.ora.com/Internet-Font-Archive.html

    @end enumerate

  4. Another good place is the Fresh Fonts I CD-ROM, there you will almost certainly find some nice fonts. The CD is available from

    @enumerate

    @item Fred Fish / Amiga Library Services (orders@amigalib.com) @item Stefan Ossowski / Schatztruhe GmbH

    @end enumerate

    The CD is for free when buying another CD from that company.

    You can also access the HTML pages on the CD under the following address:

    @enumerate

    @item http://macke.gris.informatik.uni-tuebingen.de:4711/~damor/

    @end enumerate
