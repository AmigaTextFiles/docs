Where do I get the Amiga includes?

The only legal way to get the includes and autodocs (and you should get them, they are very useful!) is to become a developer (see How do I become a developer?) or to buy the NDU (Native developers update kit, also known as NDUK or NDK), which is offered by CATS. They cost about 30$ plus shipping and this seems to me to be a fair price. See What is CATS?. The current version is 3.1.

If you need only the includes, you could get them as well with a compiler (commercial compilers only) or by getting the Fresh Fish CD. See The Fresh Fish CD-Roms.
