Dark Forces

Genre: Star Wars - 3D-Dungeon-shooter
Hardware needed: Double Speed CD-ROM, 8MB RAM, 256 color-screenmode
Software needed: System 7.1
Description: 1) "Join the Rebel Alliance`s covert operations division infiltrate the Empire, then battle every man, creature and machine the Imperial Forces can muster." - Lucas Arts
2) First person, Texture-mapped, 3D Dungeon Game. Basically it`s DOOM played in a Star-Wars Setting. You are a rebell soldier making an assault on an Imperial Base. Battle Storm Troopers as you explore the different dungeons. 8 different weaons and many scenarios. Graphics are slightly better than those found in DOOM, and you can look up and down, as well as jump.
Tested on: 1) A4000, Cyberstorm 040/40MHz, Kick/WB3.0, Cybervision64, A2091-SCSI-Controller, Toshiba XM3801B-SCSI-CD-ROM drive, 22MB RAM (10MB assigned to Shapeshifter), MacOS 7.5.5,
2) A4000 with Macro-System`s WARP ENGINE `040 running at 40 MHz Cybervision-64 (4-meg), Toshiba 6.7 speed SCSI-II CD-ROM, Amiga OS 3.1
Rating: Runs with a resolution of 320X200 VERY fast (040/40MHz/Cybervision64/20MB RAM) ! Needs 68040 (runs also on 030 but is unplayable!) Better than Doom...
Demo available: Maybe at: LucasArts
