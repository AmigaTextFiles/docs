"Why this list?"

You may know that there are not too much games especially written for the Amiga. Or think that there are about 40 or 50 new games for PC per month, but only 1 or 2 will ever be converted. The Macintosh game scene is far from being complete but some very good games will always be converted to the Mac.

So why not using SHAPESHIFTER to run the Macintosh-games that will never be available for Amiga-players? Here is the problem:

"Is my hardware good enough to play this game???"

So I`m trying to collect as much information as possible on the subject:

"What game needs what kind of hardware (RAM, processor,...)?"
