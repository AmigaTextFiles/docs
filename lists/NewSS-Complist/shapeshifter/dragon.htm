Dragon's Lair: Cartoon Video Adventure Game. Control the actions of Dirk the Daring as you try to rescue the fair princess from the clutches of an evil dragon. 100% port of the classic laser-disk game found in arcades in the middle 1980's. Gameplay consists of moving the joystick or hitting the fire button at the proper moment to keep the video playing. Entirely a game of rote memorization and trial & error. Still, if you liked the arcade version, this is a must-have! Published by ReadySoft.

Tested on:
A4000 with Macro-System's WARP ENGINE '040 running at 40 Mhz Cybervision-64 (4-meg) Toshiba 6.7 speed SCSI-II CD-ROM Amiga OS 3.1

Tested by: Adam Burns
