Pathways into Darkness

Name: Pathways into Darkness
Genre: 3D-Dungeon-shooter
Hardware needed: 4MB RAM, 256color-screenmode
Software needes: System 6.07
Description: Predecessor of Marathon (Doom-Clone)
Tested on: A4000, Cyberstorm040/40MHz, Kick/WB3.0, Cybervision64, A2091-SCSI-Controller, Toshiba XM3801B-SCSI-CD-ROM drive, 22MB RAM (10MB assigned to Shapeshifter), MacOS 7.5.5
Rating: Runs very fast on 040/40MHz/Cybervision64/20MB. Also usable with 68030/25MHz/Cybervision64 16MB. ANY Amiga-3D-Shooter is better (Nemac4, Gloom Deluxe, TKG)
Demo available: Not known.
Tested by: Andreas Etzrodt
