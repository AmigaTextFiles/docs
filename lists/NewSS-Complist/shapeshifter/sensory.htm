Sensory Overload

Sensory Overload: Ugh! This is DOOM done super-cheap! Only 3 floppy disks for the whole game. Not very pretty to look at - the graphics are very plain and all the same. Still, it`s playable in tiny resolution on a stock `040, and playable at full-screen on an accelerated 4000. Not much fun though...
Sensory Overload: First-person, Texture-mapped 3D Dungeon Game. Yet another Doom-like game, this one is not as good as the rest. You are fighting terrorists inside a large office complex. Graphics are not as detailed as other Doom-like games. Only a few different weapons are available, and the entire game fits on only 3 floppy disks. Still, it is different in that most of the fighting is done in close quarters. (Hallways and small rooms) Published by Reality Bytes.
Tested on: A4000 with Macro-System`s WARP ENGINE `040 running at 40 MHz, Cybervision-64 (4-meg), Toshiba 6.7 speed SCSI-II CD-ROM, Amiga OS 3.1
Tested by: Adam Burns
