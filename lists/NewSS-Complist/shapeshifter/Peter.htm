Peter Pan

Name: Peter Pan
Genre: Children
Hardware needed: 4MB RAM, 256 color-screenmode
Software needed: System 6.07
Description: "This revolutionary Story Painting Adventure features the Paintbox Pals, a set of animated paint tools who allow children ages 6 to 10 to control the events in the stories they read. In this adaptation of the classic tale Peter Pan, children help Peter to rescue his friends from the clutches of the evil Captain Hook. With mor than 30 scenes and over 250 possible choices, each retelling of Peter Pan is unique - limited only by a child's imagination." - Electronic Arts
