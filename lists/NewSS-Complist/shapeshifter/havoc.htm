Havoc

Havoc:

Playable on a stock 040 with reduced GFX and screen size. On an accelerated machine you can bump up the size and some of the detail, but max resolution and max detail slows it to a crawl even with a 40 MHz Warp Engine. Maybe an `060 would handle it though...

Havoc:

Tank Shoot-em-up. Fly your hovercraft close to the ground and blow up everything in your path. Lots of action. The game has very good texture-mapped backgrounds, but you need a VERY FAST machine to take advantage of this. Game also has provisions for playing head-to-head via modem. Many different missions, several different types of battle hovercraft. Published by Reality Bytes.

Tested on:
A4000 with Macro-System`s WARP ENGINE `040 running at 40 MHz Cybervision-64 (4-meg), Toshiba 6.7 speed SCSI-II CD-ROM, Amiga OS 3.1
