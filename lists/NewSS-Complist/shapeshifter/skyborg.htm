Skyborg

Skyborg: Runs just fine on the Warp Engine, the action sequences are almost impossible on a stock '040 though. Not that much fun - but I only paid $25 for it...

--------------------

SkyBorg: Adventure Game. You are a robot who must travel into the Microcosm, (a miniture universe where time is greatly accelerated) to rescue your builder, Dr. Barton. On the way, another robot, Morb, who was also built by Dr. Barton, will be trying to stop you. Morb has been malfunctioning, and is no longer sane. Gameplay is slow. Your view of the surroundings is not updated in real-time; rather, each time you take a step a new view is loaded. With very few interactive action sequences, this game is mostly about finding all the pieces you need to get your ship working, and flying around the planets of the Microcosm untill you find Dr. Barton. Published by SkyBox.

--------------------

Tested on: A4000 with Macro-System's WARP ENGINE '040 running at 40 Mhz Cybervision-64 (4-meg) Toshiba 6.7 speed SCSI-II CD-ROM Amiga OS 3.1
