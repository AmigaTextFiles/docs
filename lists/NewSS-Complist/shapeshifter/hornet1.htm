F/A-18 Hornet

Name: F/A-18 Hornet
Genre: Flight Simulator
Hardware needed: 4MB RAM, 256 color-screenmode
Software needed: System 6.07
Description: Very complex. Graphics like those in good-old "Interceptor" but 640X480 or higher.
Tested on: A4000, Cyberstorm040/40MHz, Kick/WB3.0, Cybervision64, A2091-SCSI-Controller, Toshiba XM3801B-SCSI-CD-ROM drive, 22MB RAM (10MB assigned to Shapeshifter), MacOS 7.5.5,
Rating: With 030/25MHz/16MB/Cybervision64 you will have to reduce the displaysize and number of objects.

With 040/40MHz/22MB/Cybervision64 it is good playable in 640X480 at highest detail-level.

Very smooth on A4000/060 even with two monitors.
Demo available: Don`t know
