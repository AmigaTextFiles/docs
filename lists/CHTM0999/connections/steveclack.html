       UK Call Charges - By Steve Clack (SysOp Of Total Eclipse BBS)

I've been hunting round the Internet for various Call Charges for a program
I created to calculate the cost of the Call to Users of Total Eclipse BBS.
I thought it would be a good idea to put this list in CoNnEcTiOnS too!

Basically, this is a list of Call Charges for various Telecommunication
Companies, prices are quoted in pence/min and inclusive of VAT but exclusive
of Special Offers (e.g. Friends & Family).  Plus they are the prices for
Residential use, rather than a Business Line.  Note how expensive BT is ;)
