Short : HD-Installer for Cliffhanger V1.0
Type : game/patch
Author : Bored Seal (cossick@email.cz), ICQ:21086453

This patch applies to "Cliffhanger" by Psygnosis/Spidersoft (c) 1993 - 1 disk.
Slave requires WHDLOAD (at least V7) for run.
Game requires 1.0 MB Chip Mem (+ 0.9 other memory for preload option)

FEATURES:

- Full load from HD
- NI/ROM icon (created by JHZ)
- Quit option (default key is '*' on keypad)

Thanks to rNX for disk image.

Enjoy another Psygnosis game. I enjoyed the mountain surfing on the evil terrorist
corpse :-).
