Short : HD-Installer for Magic Boy V1.0 (10-Feb-1999)
Type : game/patch
Author : Bored Seal (cossick@email.cz), ICQ:21086453

This patch applies to "Magic Boy" by Blue Turtle (c) 1993 - 1 disk.
Slave requires WHDLOAD (at least V7) for run.
Game requires 0.5 MB Chip Mem (+ 0.4 other memory for preload option)

FEATURES:

- Full load from HD
- RNC disk protection removed
- NI/MWB icon (created by JHZ, ripped by Midwik)
- Quit option (default key is '*' on keypad)

Thanks to Mark Knibbs for disk image.
