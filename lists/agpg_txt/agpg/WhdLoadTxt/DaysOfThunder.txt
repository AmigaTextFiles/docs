Short : HD-Installer for Days of Thunder V1.0
Type : game/patch
Author : Bored Seal (cossick@email.cz), ICQ:21086453

This patch applies to "Days of Thunder" by Mindscape (c) 1990 - 1 disk.
Slave requires WHDLOAD V10+.
Game requires 0.5 MB Chip Mem (+ 0.6 other memory for preload option)

FEATURES:
- Full load from HD
- Stackframe errors fixed (3x)
- NI/ROM icon (created by JHZ)
- Quit option (default key is '*' on keypad)

Thanks to Andrew David Barker for disk image and support ;).
