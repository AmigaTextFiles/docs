Short : HD-Installer for Galaxy Force 2 V1.1
Type : game/patch
Author : Bored Seal (cossick@email.cz), ICQ:21086453

This patch applies to "Galaxy Force 2" by Activision/Dementia (c) 1990 - 1 disk.
Slave requires WHDLOAD (at least V7) for run.
Game requires 0.5 MB Chip Mem (+ 0.9 other memory for preload option)

FEATURES:

- Full load from HD
- RNC disk protection removed (2x)
- ROM access fault fixed
- BUTTONWAIT tooltype added for title picture
- NI/MWB icon (created by JHZ)
- Quit option (default key is '*' on keypad)

Cheat (taken from Sweet Cheater):
  Type 'DONKEY' in game and use 'F3' for skip level function.

Thanks to Lempkee for disk image.
