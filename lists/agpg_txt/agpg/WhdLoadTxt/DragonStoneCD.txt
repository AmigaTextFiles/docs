

	That install applies to Dragon Stone CD32 (c) 1994 Core Design Ltd.


	That install requires WHDLoad to be in the path. It can be found in
	the whdload package.


	You need 1.0 MB ChipMem and 0.5 MB OtherMem.
	You need AGA chipset.
	You can quit the game by pressing F10.
