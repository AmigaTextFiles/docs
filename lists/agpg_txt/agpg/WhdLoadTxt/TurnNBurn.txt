Short : HD-Installer for Turn 'n' Burn V1.0
Type : game/patch
Author : Bored Seal (cossick@email.cz), ICQ:21086453

This patch applies to "Turn 'n' Burn" by Flair (c) 1990 - 1 disk.
Slave requires WHDLOAD V10+.
Game requires 0.5 MB Chip Mem (+ 0.9 other memory for preload option)

FEATURES:

- Full load from HD
- Disk protection removed
- Trainer option added (set CUSTOM1=1 for unlimited lives of both players)
- NI/ROM icon (created by JHZ)
- Quit option (default key is '*' on keypad)

Thanks to Helmut Motzkau for disk image and great support!
