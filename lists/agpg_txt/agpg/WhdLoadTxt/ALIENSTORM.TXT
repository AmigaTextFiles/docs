Type : game/patch

         Keith Krellwitz (abaddon@wwa.com)

This patch applies to "Alien Storm" by Sega/US Gold (c) 1990 - 1 disk.
Slave requires WHDLOAD (at least V7) for run.
Game requires 1.0 MB Chip Mem (+ 0.7 other memory for preload option)

FEATURES:

- Full load from HD
- Disk protection removed
- Stackframe error fixed (by Keith)
- Trainer option (set CUSTOM1=1 for unlimited credits)
- NI/MWB icon (created by JHZ)
- Quit option (default key is '*' on keypad)

Thanks to Mark Knibbs for disk image.
