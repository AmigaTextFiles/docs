Short : HD-Installer for Back To The Future 2 V1.1 (24-Dec-1998)
Type : game/patch
Author : Bored Seal (cossick@email.cz), ICQ:21086453

This patch applies to "Back To The Future 2" by Image Works/Images (c) 1990 - 1 disk.
Slave requires WHDLOAD (at least V7.0) for run.
Game requires 0.5 MB Chip Mem (+ 0.9 other memory for preload option)

FEATURES:

- Full load from HD
- RNC disk protection removed
- Trainer option (set CUSTOM1=1 for unlimited lives)
- NI/MWB icon (created by JHZ, ripped by Midwik)
- Quit option (default key is '*' on keypad)

Thanks to Lempkee for disk image.
