This installer requires the original Matrix Marauders disk from Psygnosis.

Still has a slight gfx bug in weapons display on an 060, but I will fix this
soon.

Features:

- JST and WHDLoad loaders
- System friendly imager (requires RawDic v1.6)
- At any time press 'F10' to quit.
- Scores saved to HD
- Saved games to HD
