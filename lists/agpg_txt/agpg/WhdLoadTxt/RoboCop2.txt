

	That install applies to "RoboCop 2" (C) 1990 Ocean Software Ltd.


	That install requires WHDLoad to be in the path. It can be found in
	the whdload package.


	You need x.x MB ChipMem and 0.5 MB OtherMem.
	You can quit the game by pressing ESC.

	As a special feature the highscores will be saved now.
