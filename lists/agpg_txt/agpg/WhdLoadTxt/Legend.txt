
	Legend HD Fix by Tue Brisson (v1.1)


	Before you can use this you must install WHDLoad
	from the whdload package. (DIC is needed to get savefiles.)
	The tools must be available via the path.


	Required memory:	0.5 MB ChipMem
				0.5 MB FastMem

	To quit press:		DEL


	Notes: Now you can set the MMU tooltype.
	Preload also works properly now.
