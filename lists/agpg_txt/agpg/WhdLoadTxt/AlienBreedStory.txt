Short : HD-Installer for Alien Breed Story Disk V1.0
Type : game/patch
Author : Bored Seal (cossick@email.cz), ICQ:21086453

This patch applies to "Alien Breed Story Disk" by Team 17 (c) 1991 - 1 disk.
It supports Story Disk from original (3 disks) Alien Breed release.
Slaves require WHDLOAD V10+.
Intro requires 1.0 MB Chip Mem (+ 0.9 other memory for preload option)

FEATURES:

- Full loading from HD
- Decruncher relocated to Fast RAM
- ROM access replaced by normal quit to OS
- Stack error fixed
- NI/ROM icon (created by JHZ)
- Quit option (default key is '*' on keypad)

Thanks to Helmut Motzkau for disk image.
