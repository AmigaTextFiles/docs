;--- Fears HD install V3.1 [registered users only]

This installer requires the two original Fears disks. It supports three versions.
The UK one and two that were sold in Germany (Attic label).

You have to install WHDLoad first.

You need 2MB chip and 1.5MB fast.

If you have not yet registered WHDLoad, this patch will NOT work. You will return
to the workbench right after the trainer options.
