Short : HD-Installer for Necronom V1.0
Type : game/patch
Author : Bored Seal (cossick@email.cz), ICQ:21086453

This patch applies to "Necronom" by LineL (c) 1990 - 1 disk.
Slave requires WHDLOAD V10+.
Game requires 0.5 MB Chip Mem (+ 0.9 other memory for preload option)

FEATURES:

- Full load from HD
- Access faults fixed (2x)
- Blitter waits inserted
- Highscores are saved
- NI/ROM icon (created by JHZ, ripped by Midwik)
- Quit option (your default key)

Thanks to Helmut Motzkau and Condor for disk image.
