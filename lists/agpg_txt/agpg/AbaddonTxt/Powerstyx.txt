Short:	 	HD Installer for Powerstyx v1.1
Author:		Keith Krellwitz (abaddon@wwa.com)
Uploader:	Keith Krellwitz (abaddon@wwa.com)
Type:	 	game/patch

This installer requires the original Powerstyx disk from DigiTek Software.

Thanks to Chris Vella for the PAL version.

It has been successfully tested on a A1200/030/40MHz/10MB and
A4000T/060/50MHz/48MB.

Features:

 - JST and WHDLoad loaders.
 - Two versions supported
 - System friendly imager (Requires RawDic v1.6)
 - Press 'F10' to quit.
 - Saves scores to HD

CHEAT: Press 'CTRL' on the title screen for infinite lives.  The JST version
will not quit most of the time if you use the trainer.
