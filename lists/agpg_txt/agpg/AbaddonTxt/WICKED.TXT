Author:		Keith Krellwitz (abaddon@wwa.com)
Uploader:	Keith Krellwitz (abaddon@wwa.com)
Type:	 	game/patch

This installer requires the original Wicked disk.

It has been successfully tested on a A1200/030/40MHz/10MB and on an
A4000t/060/50MHz/48MB.

The JST loader does not work correctly on the 060, but the WHDLoad version
works on all.

Features:

- JST and WHDLoad loaders
- Press 'F10' at any time to quit.
