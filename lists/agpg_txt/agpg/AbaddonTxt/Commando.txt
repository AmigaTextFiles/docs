>>>>>>> REQUIRES A FULL RE-INSTALL!!!!!!!!!!!!

It has been successfully tested on a A1200/030/40MHz/10MB and
A4000T/060/50MHz/48MB.

Features:

 - JST and WHDLoad loaders.
 - System friendly imager (Requires RawDic v1.6)
 - Press 'F10' to quit.
 - Fixed 2 access faults
 - Saves scores to HD
 - Trainer - infinite lives and gernades - no highscores saved
      WHDLoad: set the Custom1 tooltype > 0
      JST: set the TRAINER tooltype
