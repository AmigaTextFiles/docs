This installer requires the original Beavers from Grandslam.

A major thanks to Harry for all his help with system friendly disk
imagers.

Fixed:

- fixed 24 bit addressing problem
- fixed two access faults

The game should now runn on all amigas.

Features:

 - JST and WHDLoad versions
 - Press 'F10' to quit.
 - System friendly imager.
 - Decode routine in fastmem.
