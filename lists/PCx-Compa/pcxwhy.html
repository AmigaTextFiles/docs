Why this list

With your Amiga you can do alot things. For nearly everything you can get software. So why should you get a pc-emulator and use pc-software? I think, just for fun, or to impress your friends. And there were still some kinds of programs, that were only available for PC.

But how could you know what software runs on the emulator and what not? Here is the solution! This list is for the PCx emulator from Microcode Solutions.

I found two lists for Shapeshifter, but no for PCx (ok, there is a small on the HAAGE-Partner support-page). So I decided to make my own. I hope it can help you and please help me to enhance it. It depends on YOU (I know, that everybody says that ;-)).
