Moonlight BBS offers a large collection of files in over 40 subject areas.
Unlimited downloads (no ratios) for registered & validated callers.
New files are added frequently from recent Aminet uploads.
The BBS caters mainly for the 'serious' caller who wants useful files:
but we have a small stock of games, pictures & animations, music files.
Newcomers to the 'comms' scene are given help and advice.
There are a number of 'help text' files available for download.

Special features
----------------
Bulletins (Information and help texts.)
Online game ('Hack & Slash'), 'Trivial Pursuit' type quiz.
Online CD: the latest Aminet CD is always available to download files.
SysOp Chat - keyboard 'chat' with the SysOp when he is available.
Callers' advertisements: For Sale and Wanted (No charge for adverts.)
Tutorial files for comms & internet software setup.
