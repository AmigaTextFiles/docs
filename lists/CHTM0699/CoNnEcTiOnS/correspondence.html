"Love CoNnEcTiOnS, it's very comprehensive and very professional.  How
about creating a CoNnEcTiOnS PC Executable rather than just the Text-File
PC Version?"

Ian Jones "Intruder" (Barnsley, South Yorkshire)

Editors Comment:-

CoNnEcTiOnS is constructed entirely on an AMIGA A1200-030 using CygnusED
Professional, and therefore the problem of making a PC Executable of
CoNnEcTiOnS arises, as CoNnEcTiOnS does not have access to a PC, and
indeed if we had, we probably wouldn't know where to start in making a PC
Executable!!!  If there are PC Users out there, who read CoNnEcTiOnS, then
we openly invite anyone to create a PC Executable, as long as they confirm
the project with CoNnEcTiOnS first, as CoNnEcTiOnS IS copyrighted!!!
We are currently looking into the possibility of releasing CoNnEcTiOnS in
HTML Format, which would mean that CoNnEcTiOnS could be read on any System
with an Internet Web-Browser.  This is as close as CoNnEcTiOnS can get at
this moment in time to a PC Executable.
