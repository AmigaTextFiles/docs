Wolfpack



WolfPack: Submarine Simulator. World War II German U-Boats are at your command. Switch to any boat in the WolfPack of up to 6 boats. Control the subs as you try to sink allied shipping. Watch out for Destroyers! Good graphics and very involved Gameplay. Much more complicated than Silent Service. Published by Nova Logic.
