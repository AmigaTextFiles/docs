Greetings...

POWERNET is a *NEW* up and coming Echo-Mailnet to hit your screens, with
JUST a few months of running, we have managed to persuade many people to
connect a feed.  This Mailnet has 9 Mail Sections, hopefully to cater for
ALL your mailing needs.

POWERNET was specifically designed to have an anything goes attitude, there
are NO Moderators stamping on your toes at every opportunity.

We also have a Web-Site to compliment POWERNET, designed by my Co-SysOp,
Co-Founder and Nodelist Co-Ordinator, Chris Biggs (SysOp Of Hellfire BBS).
The POWERNET Web-Site can be found at:-

http://www.power-net.freeserve.co.uk

OUR AIM is to offer as many feeds to POWERNET as possible, giving POWERNET
the chance to grow and prosper.  We are aiming to put POWERNET on the market
as THE PLACE to be and where ALL the ACTION is.

ALSO, FREE (Yes...I said FREE) Adverts of YOUR BBS on our POWERNET Web-Site.
This service will be of NO cost to you, all we ask is JOIN TODAY!!!

In addition to your BBS Advert being added to our POWERNET Web-Site, we
will also add a link to your Web-Site (If Applicable).  This Advert service
will add support to YOUR BBS and Web-Site...
