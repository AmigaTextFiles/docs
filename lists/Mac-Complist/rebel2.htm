Rebel Assault II - The Hidden Empire



Emulator: Shapeshifter
Genre: Action
Name: Rebell Assault II - The Hidden Empire
Description: Two Cds for 15 game-levels with stunning Full Motion Videos. You delivered the fatal blow to the Death Star, but there is no time to celebrate. Darth Vader wants revenge and is on a galaxy-wide hunt for a lethal weapon. He is determined to destroy the Rebel Alliance.

Game play for various chapters follows one of this four models:
- COCKPIT FLIGHT, moving your controller moves your ship and aims your target sight.
- GROUND COMBAT, use your controller to move your gun, aiming it at enemies.
- BEHIND-SHIP FLIGHT, pull your controller back to rise, forward to fall, or left or right to bank.
- POINT-OF-VIEW COMBAT, use your controller to move your body and to look/aim at targets; press the Fire button to fire your lasers.
min. Configuration: - 25MHz 68040
- Graphic card (256 colour)
- Double speed (2x) or faster CD-ROM drive
- 8 MB RAM
- System 7.1 or higher
Tested on: A4000/040-25MHz Cybervision 64, CD-ROM 4x, 18MB RAM
A4000/060-50MHz PPC604e-200MHz, Cybervision 64, CD-ROM 4x, 50MB RAM
Ratings: Good enough on 68040/25 (slightly slow), but perfect on 68060/50 (absolutly fantastic).
