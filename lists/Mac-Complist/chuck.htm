Chuck Yeager`s Air Combat



Chuck Yeager's Air Combat: Works in 16 color mode on a stock '040, runs great in full-color mode on an accelerated '040.

--------------------

Chuck Yeager's Air Combat: Flight Simulator. Fly World War II and Vietnam War era planes in realistic dogfights against classic German and soviet planes. Very basic graphics. Realistic flight controls, different performance specs for each plane, totally configurable missions. Almost limitless possible missions. Not very easy to fly from the keyboard, but it's possible. Published by Electronic Arts.

Tested on:
A4000 with Macro-System's WARP ENGINE '040 running at 40 Mhz Cybervision-64 (4-meg) Toshiba 6.7 speed SCSI-II CD-ROM Amiga OS 3.1

Tested by: Adam Burns
