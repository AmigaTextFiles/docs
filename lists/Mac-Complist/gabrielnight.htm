Gabriel Knight - The Beast within



Emulator: Shapeshifter
Genre: Adventure
Name: Gabriel Knight - The Beast within
Description: You are slipping into the Role either of Gabriel as of Grace, who travel to Munich, to solve a serial of murders, where the mutilations of the victims seem to be the work of a Werewolf. Gabriel has to face his own demons while Grace is on the trace of a historical secret - the mysterical death of the insane bavarian King Ludwig II. the Hunters get hunted themselves and the only hope of redemption requires to take desperate measures...

The Game is a kind of interactive movie.
min. Configuration: 68040/33 MHz, System 7.1, 16 MB RAM, 3 MB Harddiskspace, DoubleSpeed CD, 630x480 256 Colors
Tested on: A 4000 with 060/50 MHz, 10 x CD ROM, Cybervision 64/3D
Ratings: Nice game with 6 CD`s. The Gameplay is kind of boring because all you have to do is to klick objects in the right way. The Animations are filmed and digitized which gives the game a touch of reality.
For the AMIGA though it`s worth a buy because it`s the only game this way and even of it`s easy structure better than any game on the AMIGA except the very new Myst now.
