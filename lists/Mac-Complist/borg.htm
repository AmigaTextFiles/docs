Name: StarTrek: Borg
Description: Remember Wolf 394? The attack of the Borg Cube which destroyed mosts ships of the biggest armada ever?
Yes, you do - your father died there a couple of years ago. You are a StarFleet cadett, serving on a StarFleet's ship and the Borg are back again! Time for revenge? Naah, your captain evacates all unnecessary personal, and you have to go, too. Then Q appears and offers you to travel back in time to kill some Borg and save your father's life...

This game is a MUST for all Trekkies all over the world. Its an Interactive Movie based on the StarTrek series The Next Generation. At certain points you have to make decisions, which will involve the story. Getting borgified never been so much fun...
It comes on 3 CDs; a bonus is the Picard Dossier on CD2, which presents valuable infos about the main protagonists of StarTrek in generally.
