Super Wing Commander - Shoot `em up



Super Wing Commander should work but: with Shapeshifter 3.3 it works but only if I change my CD-ROM from "DIRECT DMA" to "DIREKT POLLING" (Fastlane Z3 - look at the Shapeshifter-docs for an explanation). For that reason the movie-sequences are *very* slow.

With Shapeshifter 3.6 the CD-ROM works as normal but the game crashes...

Tested with:

  • Amiga 4000 Rev 3.1
  • CyberStorm 060/50MHz MK1
  • Fastlane Z3
  • CyberVision 64 / 4MB
  • 48MB FastRAM
  • 2MB ChipRAM
  • Kickstart 3.0
  • Workbench 3.1
  • Toshiba 4x CD-ROM XM-5401TA
