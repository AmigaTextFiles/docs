Civilization II



Emulator: Fusion + Shapeshifter
Genre: Strategic
Name: Civilization II
Description: Civilization....the most famous strategic game in the whole world....here in its second incarnation. In this game your control a whole civilization and try to let it survive from 3000 B.C. to the Space era conquering the world in the meanwhile. You can build cities, research new discoveries that go from the Wheel to the Superconductors, buld military units and conquer the world or build a spaceship and colonize Alpha Centauri!
This second Chapter features more discoveries, more buildings, more Wonders, more units, better graphics, a more advanced diplomatic system and a refinated combat engine (no more Armor lost to a single phalanx!)
min. Configuration: 68040 25Mhz, 2x CD-Rom (IDE or SCSI), 256 colours graphic and MacOS 7.01
Tested on: 68060 50Mhz, Cybervision 64, about 20MB of emulated RAM, Fusion V.2.0, 12x SCSI CD-Rom, 16x IDE CD-Rom
N.B.: Shapeshifter V 3.8 refuse to run this about 75% of the times; you need a SCSI CD-Rom to listen to the digital music. On this configuration the game run fine and its faster than CivAGA on my Amiga!
Ratings: 9/10, I`ve nowords to describe the masterpiece of all the software games, I gabe it only 9 becaue it isn`t so different from Civilization.
Demo available: Yes, but it`s BIG!
