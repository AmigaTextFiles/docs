                                Introduction

Hello CoNnEcTiOnS Readers, and welcome to my first review column.  Dunkie
(SysOp Of Quest BBS) has been kind enough to let me write a few reviews for
connections in this month's edition.  I don't know if it'll be a regular
thing, just let him or me know what you think and we'll see what happens.

Anyway here's how it'll work provided you like it and it keeps going.  I'm
going to give an overall rating of the Music Module out of 10.  This rating
is governed by a few factors, these being - Commercial Viability: This is
whether I and some of my Music Industry contacts think it could be vinyl
worthy.  Style & Groove: This is whether the Music Module actually has taste
and style, be it "Of The Moment" or not, and Sampling & Timing:  This is the
most important, if it ain't in tune or out of time then you've failed!!!

Also I'll try and give a list of where to get the Music Modules, but they'll
all be available at Innocent BBS, so you can download them there or upload
any submissions you want me to review, or just give my personal opinion on.
If you do want to submit something, then please make it something I can
listen to i.e one of the more popular Music Module formats.  I don't want to
be converting Music Modules of all sorts, please do it for me.  Plus if
there are any specific Music Modules or styles you would like mentioned or 
reviewed, then let me or Dunkie (SysOp Of Quest BBS) know.

All the Music Modules reviewed have been played through a Stereo, to give
maximum effect and I suggest you do the same!!!

Hendrix (SysOp Of Innocent BBS)
