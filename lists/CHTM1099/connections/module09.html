   Electronica: Music Module Reviews - By Hendrix (SysOp Of Innocent BBS)

  Module Title: "Maniac"
 Module Author: Celsius
  Module Style: Hip-Hop
Available From: AMINET (mods/cels/maniac.lha)

Celsius is fast turning out to be a favourite of mine, but if you like
quality then you will understand.  Here we have a phenomenal Hip-Hop effort,
with absolutely astounding quality voice samples.  Again, I can't fault this
tune for a single thing, the mix is of studio quality and stylistically,
it's groovin.  I bet you tap your feet to at least 20 seconds of it!!!  The
timing is spot on especially for drums and voice samples mixed together,
plus there is a good mixture of samples and tonal differences to keep you
nodding your head to the beat.

Commercial Viability: 5/5
      Style & Groove: 5/5
   Sampling & Timing: 5/5

       Module Rating: 9/10

Hendrix (SysOp Of Innocent BBS)
