   Electronica: Music Module Reviews - By Hendrix (SysOp Of Innocent BBS)

  Module Title: <Unnamed>
 Module Author: <Unnamed>
  Module Style: Euro Dance
Available From: AMINET (mods/med/evolut.lha)

The very mention of the name "Euro Dance" almost makes me throw up, but in
the interests of fair criticism, here's one for the Euro Dance fans.  OK...
so we've all heard one like this before, but for an AMIGA attempt, it's not
bad, and it has enough changes for me.  The weird bit is, I'm sure that if
it had vocals it would have been on every "Best of Euro Dance" album in the
country last summer, and all those silly little boy racers would have bought
it...fools!!!  Anyway, if you like a bit of Euro Dance, then try this one.
Bad points: it has a kind of weird out of tune synth that gets a bit
annoying.

Commercial Viability: 4/5
      Style & Groove: 3/5
   Sampling & Timing: 4/5

       Module Rating: 7/10
