   Electronica: Music Module Reviews - By Hendrix (SysOp Of Innocent BBS)

  Module Title: Discipline D U B
 Module Author: <Unnamed>
  Module Style: Dub/Drum & Bass/Jungle
Available From: AMINET (mods/misc/girl-dub.lha)

Ever started listening to a tune for the first time, then just as you are 
about to turn it off it hits you right in the face with something you
wouldn't have expected, well this is one of those ones.  It just blew me
away, I mean everybody knows what Dub is, but for me, I tend to find it hard
to get my head around it, even after playing in a Ska band for nearly two
years!!!  But if you want a good idea of what Dub is, then give this a
listen, it has also got something for you Drum & Bass freaks out there.  I
suppose you could call it a sort of Dub to Drum & Bass crossover.

Commercial Viability: 3/5
      Style & Groove: 4/5
   Sampling & Timing: 4/5

       Module Rating: 9/10
