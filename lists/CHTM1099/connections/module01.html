   Electronica: Music Module Reviews - By Hendrix (SysOp Of Innocent BBS)

  Module Title: Too_Fast
 Module Author: Celsius
  Module Style: Kickin Drum & Bass/Jungle
Available From: AMINET (mods/cels/TooFast.lha)

OK, let's see.  I'm not a Drum & Bass or Jungle fan but this Module is
kicking, a shining example of what can be done with only 4 tracks.  This
Module has serious commercial viability, it has all the hooks and cuts of a
mainstream 12", plus lots of groove and changes so as not to let you loose
your interest.  The timing is superbly tuned for a Module and the voice
sampling is well chosen and clear.  I really can't fault it much at all,
except that it does have a certain amount of repetativeness, but all its
great melodies and hooks far outway those bad points.

Commercial Viability: 4/5
      Style & Groove: 5/5
   Sampling & Timing: 5/5

       Module Rating: 8/10

Hendrix (SysOp Of Innocent BBS)
