   Electronica: Music Module Reviews - By Hendrix (SysOp Of Innocent BBS)

  Module Title: City Air
 Module Author: <Unnamed>
  Module Style: Funk/Acid Jazz/Easy Listening
Available From: AMINET (mods/funk/cityair.lha)

For a funk Module with guitar and drums that aren't loops, this is a very
well done Module.  The samples are well mixed and chosen, and the content is
almost vinyl worthy.  If it had vocals and the instruments were real, it
could almost sound as if it was one of the Brand New Heavies' tunes.  I warn
you however if egotistical instrumentals are not your style, then keep well
clear of this one.

Commercial Viability: 4/5
      Style & Groove: 4/5
   Sampling & Timing: 4/5

       Module Rating: 8/10
