   Electronica: Music Module Reviews - By Hendrix (SysOp Of Innocent BBS)

  Module Title: <Unnamed>
 Module Author: <Unnamed>
  Module Style: Drum & Bass/Jungle
Available From: AMINET (mods/jungl/dominion.lha)

This one is very repetative, but it has a real dark feeling to it and has a
brilliant filtering effect throughout it, which I hardly ever hear used in
the correct way, but it's used here to a give a great effect.  It falls
short on the content, getting a bit bare at times, but when it kicks it
really does kick.  The minus points are: it could have used a little mixing
just to perk the whole thing up a bit, and it is a little bare, but I think
the bare parts are in context with the overall feel of the tune.  Check it
out though...not a bad one.

Commercial Viability: 2/5
      Style & Groove: 3/5
   Sampling & Timing: 4/5

       Module Rating: 6/10
